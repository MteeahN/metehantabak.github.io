Growing stronger through movement and learning.

Problem & Solution

Making AI that feels alive without killing performance.

The Challenge
The core challenge was expanding accessibility and awareness while preserving the academy’s educational values and personal connection with families.
Reaching more children beyond physical location limitations
Clearly informing parents about educational programs and developmental benefits
Simplifying communication between families and the academy
Enabling remote appointment booking without manual coordination
Building digital trust while maintaining a warm, child-centered identity
The Solution
The result is a digital platform that increases accessibility, strengthens parent communication, and supports sustainable institutional growth for Hareketli Gelişim Akademisi.
Developed a responsive, SEO-optimized website to expand digital reach
Structured programs into clear, parent-friendly content sections
Integrated detailed program descriptions highlighting developmental outcomes
Implemented secure online contact and remote appointment booking forms
Designed a warm, modern interface aligned with child development principles
Built a scalable content structure for continuous growth and program expansion
Development Journey

A structured multi-phase development process.

Phase 01
Research & Architecture
The project began with analyzing institutional needs, defining target audiences (students and parents), and designing the site architecture. Program categories, navigation structure, and content hierarchy were planned to ensure clarity and accessibility. A scalable MVC-based backend structure was prepared to support long-term updates and expansion.
PlanningUX StructureSystem Architecture
Phase 02
Core Website Development
The visual identity was translated into a modern UI design with responsive layouts. Program pages, about sections, and contact modules were implemented. Dynamic form handling was integrated with backend validation to ensure secure and structured data submission.
Frontend DevelopmentResponsive DesignForm Integration
Phase 03
Optimization & Deployment
The final stage focused on performance tuning, SEO optimization, and usability refinement. Loading speeds were improved, structured metadata was implemented, and mobile responsiveness was polished. The site was prepared for production deployment with a clean, maintainable codebase.
